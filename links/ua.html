Chair Pose (Utkatasana)

  • Stand straight with your feet slightly apart.
  • Stretch your hands to the front with palms facing downwards. Do not bend your elbows.
  • Bend the knees as if you are sitting in an imaginary chair.
  • Be comfortable.
  • Ensure that you keep your hands parallel to the ground. Relax in that pose for some time.

  • Come back up and stand straight or you may lie down on your back and relax.
