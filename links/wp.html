Warrior Pose (Veerabhadrasana)

  • Stand straight with your legs wide apart.
  • Turn your right foot out by 90 degrees and left foot in by about 15 degrees.
  • Lift both arms sideways to shoulder height with your palms facing upwards. Arms should be parallel to the ground.
  • Breathing out, bend your right knee.
  • Turn your head and look to your right.Stretch your arms further.
  • Hold the yoga posture.
  • Breathing in, come up.
  • Breathing out, bring your hands down from the sides.
  • Repeat the same for the left side (turn your left foot out by 90 degrees and turn the right foot in by about 15 degrees).
