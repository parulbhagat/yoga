Yoga does more than burn calories and tone muscles. It's a total mind-body workout that combines strengthening and stretching poses with deep breathing and meditation or relaxation. Some of its benefits include:

  • Improves your flexibility
  • Builds muscle strength
  • Perfects your posture
  • Betters your bone health
  • Increases your blood flow
  • Boosts your immune system functionality
  • Drops your blood pressure
  • Lowers blood sugar
  • Helps you focus
  • Relaxes your system
  • Gives you inner strength
